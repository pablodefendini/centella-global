**Option A — Contemporary.** Warm-paper, navy-ink, ultramarine and neon-yellow. Inter Tight + Inter + JetBrains Mono. Foundation-rigorous with high-voltage moments. The brochure as a current-issue magazine.

**Option B — Spring Bloom.** Soft cream, deep aubergine ink, five pastel hues — cherry blossom, fresh leaf, robin's-egg sky, wisteria, daffodil. Lora + Source Serif 4 + Inter. Affectionate and hopeful; the twentieth as rebirth. The brochure as a botanical print run for grown-ups.

**Option C — Heritage / WWP 2001.** Warm cream paper, navy display ink (`#1B3A52`, the &ldquo;women waging peace&rdquo; headline color), orange and amber band fills lifted from the original 2001 *Women Waging Peace* program book. Cormorant Garamond + Inter. Single-page format, ornamental frame, Roman dates, italic ampersand in amber. The brochure as a direct visual quotation of Hunt&rsquo;s heritage program identity — the twentieth by way of its origin.

**Option D — Hunt Compass.** White paper, Hunt-brown ink, a ten-color compass (teal, sky, mint, green, yellow, orange, red, coral, pink, purple — Hunt brand teal absorbs the previous WP-default blue, so the compass keeps a single brand-true blue). Open Sans throughout. Logo-led: the Swanee Hunt mark is the cover device. The brochure as Hunt Alternatives' house publication.

The Hunt logo appears in every direction (cover-mark or cover-meta in A and B, title page in C, cover device in D).

All four pass WCAG AAA on body text. Every accent fill carries dark-on-light or light-on-dark text at AA-AAA. Body and accent contrast ratios are documented in each option's bottom legend.

## Format decisions (locked)

- **Trim:** 6×9 portrait, saddle-stitched, ~28–36pp depending on bio length.
- **Pocket:** printed pocket on the inside back cover, holds a separately-printed agenda insert. Lets the bulk of the book go to the printer early while the agenda stays fluid until the morning of.
- **Cut from print:** Hunt Alternatives organizational description, Swanee's bio.
- **Identity lead:** Hunt Alternatives / Prime Movers. Centella as producer acknowledged in the colophon only (not on cover) unless Swanee directs otherwise.
- **Visual motifs:** boxes (Centella panel pattern — outer rule, inner frame, hard edges, 4–8px radius depending on option) and quote callouts sprinkled throughout.

## Design system note

This brochure does **not** use Centella's own design system (the dark, "architectural and assertive" identity defined in `/design.md`). It is for a client (Hunt Alternatives), and the four directions are calibrated to Swanee Hunt's audience and tone, not Centella's.

Body text clears WCAG AAA on every option (12.8–18.2:1). Bright fills are deployed with dark text on top; deep fills with light text on top; accent text in headlines uses deepened on-brand variants for AA-AAA at small sizes. Centella's identity stays in the colophon only. If Swanee directs Centella's producer credit forward, that's a separate conversation.
