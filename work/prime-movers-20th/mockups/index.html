Three design directions.

Three directions for the Prime Movers & the Next Generation reunion brochure — Prime Movers & the Next Generation, Meeting the Moment, the Twentieth. Each option carries the same section list and the same content slots; what varies is typography, palette, and structural register.

What you're looking at, and what you're not. All copy in these mockups is placeholder — including participant names. Bios, welcomes, the thought piece, even the names in the bio grids are stand-ins until real content lands. Nothing here is typeset for production. These are layout and typography studies meant to be reacted to, not proofed.
Palette revision — bright Centella, light grounds. Each option now sources its color from the Centella token system (paired families on light variants), kept to two color families per composition. Paper grounds are pale tints (lavender, warm cream, pink); brights (violet, coral, pink, orange) are deployed as fills only — accent text is dark-on-bright or family-deepened to keep WCAG 2.1 AA on every block. Body copy clears AAA (≥14:1) on all three.

What's the same in all three

Trim & format. 6×9 portrait, saddle-stitched, ~28–36pp depending on bio length. Pocket on the inside back cover holds a separately-printed agenda insert — lets the body of the book go to the printer early while the agenda stays fluid until morning of.
