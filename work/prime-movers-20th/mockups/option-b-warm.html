Conduct❦28
  1. i.
    Confidentiality
    What is shared in the room stays in the room. Names, attributions, and specifics do not leave with you.
  2. ii.
    Generous attention
    Listen before responding. Assume the speaker has spent more time on the question than you have.
  3. iii.
    Disagreement, not disrespect
    Push hard on the idea, never on the person. Your strongest critique should land as a gift.
  4. iv.
    One mic at a time
    No side conversations during plenaries. The room only works if everyone is in it.
  5. v.
    Care for the convening
    Phones away, devices down, presence on. Twenty years has earned us the right to be undistracted.

Option B — at a glance

Direction
Warm / human, brightened. Relationship-forward, twenty-years-of-people, the convening of friends rather than a convening of a board. Closest reference: Aperture, Magnum photo books, the warmer end of foundation publications — but in spring colors.
Type pairing
Lora (display, italic-forward) + Source Serif 4 (body) + Inter (eyebrows, labels). Italic does much of the expressive work.
Palette
Centella networking + global. Warm cream paper (--global-light #FFF3E1), deep wine ink (--networking-dark #22181C) — body text passes WCAG AAA at 15.8:1. Coral (--networking #FF6B6B) and bright orange (--global #FF9500) used as fills: cover sunset wash, bio-photo duotone, urgent-row gradient, venue pill, headline emphasis swatches. Decorative open-quote glyphs at 56–96px sit in coral as typographic ornament. Ornamental ❦ marks at structural breaks.
Two-family rule
The earlier draft mixed three families (networking + global + a stray violet "teal"); this revision drops the violet to comply with Centella's "no more than two color families" guidance. Networking and global only.
Signatures
Photo-led cover with sunset gradient + text plate beneath. Italic display set throughout. Italic emphasis words sit in coral swatches with dark ink. Bio "Joined · year" detail. Lowercase italic numerals on the conduct list.
Boxes
Centella panel pattern adapted to the warm aesthetic: 8px outer radius, coral inner frame at 40% opacity, soft warm gradients on quote and urgent boxes. Practical info uses a vertical stack, each row a small framed panel — feels hand-set rather than tabular.
Quotes
Sprinkled in three places — thought piece (right), bios (left), conduct (left). Each opens with a large coral quotation mark over dark ink body; warmth of the type carries the affect.
What participants will feel
Affection, with sunshine. The 20-year mark as the people in the room — not the institution that convened them. Closer to a spring yearbook for grown-ups than a program book.
