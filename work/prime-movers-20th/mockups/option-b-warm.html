Conduct❦28
  1. i.
    Confidentiality
    What is shared in the room stays in the room. Names, attributions, and specifics do not leave with you.
  2. ii.
    Generous attention
    Listen before responding. Assume the speaker has spent more time on the question than you have.
  3. iii.
    Disagreement, not disrespect
    Push hard on the idea, never on the person. Your strongest critique should land as a gift.
  4. iv.
    One mic at a time
    No side conversations during plenaries. The room only works if everyone is in it.
  5. v.
    Care for the convening
    Phones away, devices down, presence on. Twenty years has earned us the right to be undistracted.

Option B — Spring Bloom · pastel rebirth

Direction
Warm / human, in pastels. Cherry blossoms, new leaves, robin's-egg sky — twenty years as rebirth, not a march. Affectionate, hopeful, garden-soft. Closest reference: a spring yearbook, Magnum photo books in their lighter editions, foundation publications that pick up an Arts & Letters lightness.
Type pairing
Lora (display, italic-forward) + Source Serif 4 (body) + Inter (eyebrows, labels). Italic does the expressive work; the pastel fills do the warmth.
Palette
Pastel Spring. Soft cream paper (#FBF7F0) with blush secondary (#F5E8E4). Deep aubergine ink (#2C2A4A) — body text passes WCAG AAA at 12.8:1. Five pastel fills: cherry blossom #F8C8D5, fresh leaf #C5DDB8, robin's-egg sky #B8DCEB, wisteria #D4C5E8, daffodil #FAE5A1 — each carries ink text at 8-11:1 AAA. Accent text on paper uses deep variants (blossom-deep #883A5C, leaf-deep #4A7341, sky-deep #2D6A87, wisteria-deep #6E4D9C, buttercup-deep #7A5C00) — all AA, most AAA.
Two-family rule
The pastels share a common value (high luminance, low saturation) so they read as a single bouquet rather than five competing colors. Blossom and leaf carry the load; sky / wisteria / buttercup are extensions for variety.
Signatures
Cover-photo gradient is a spring sunrise with a bolder middle: blossom → orchid → buttercup. Italic display throughout. Italic emphasis words sit in blossom swatches with deep aubergine ink. Bio thumbnails are a blossom-and-buttercup wash.
Boxes
Centella panel pattern in pastels: 8px outer radius, blossom-deep inner hairline at low opacity (mauve, soft on cream), watercolor-style gradients on quote and urgent boxes. Practical info uses a vertical stack — each row a small framed panel.
Quotes
Three placements — thought piece, bios, conduct. Each opens with a large blossom-pink quotation glyph; ink body text carries the message.
What participants will feel
Twenty years as rebirth. Spring, not a survey. Closer to a botanical print run for grown-ups than a program book — warm without being sentimental, hopeful without being saccharine.
