Conduct28
1
Confidentiality
What is shared in the room stays in the room. Names, attributions, and specifics do not leave with you.
2
Generous attention
Listen before responding. Assume the speaker has spent more time on the question than you have.
3
Disagreement, not disrespect
Push hard on the idea, never on the person. Your strongest critique should land as a gift.
4
One mic at a time
No side conversations during plenaries. The room only works if everyone is in it.
5
Care for the convening
Phones away, devices down, presence on. Twenty years has bought us the right to be undistracted.

Option A — at a glance

Direction
Contemporary / progressive. Reads as forward-looking, current-movement, modern philanthropic publishing — warm paper, navy ink, ultramarine fills, neon-yellow pop. Closest reference: Aperture, MIT Press, contemporary foundation reports — but with a punk-yearbook electric streak.
Type pairing
Inter Tight (display, heavy weights for impact) + Inter (body) + JetBrains Mono (eyebrows, folios, technical labels).
Palette
B-* tokens. Warm off-white paper (#F2F1ED) and deeper warm neutral (#E5E3DC) for box fills. Near-black with deep navy cast (#0A0E2C) for ink — body text passes WCAG AAA at 16.7:1 on paper. Ultramarine (#1B2A8C) carries accent labels, em phrases and key blocks; lighter ultramarine (#2C3FB8) is the secondary blue. Neon yellow (#E8FF3A) is the high-voltage pop — small chips, the welcome panel, the urgent row.
Fill patterns
Two layered fill behaviors. --B-ultra is a deep navy and takes cream-paper text on top (cover-band, accent module, panel--accent, pocket page, quote--invert, headline accent chips). --B-pop is a bright neon-yellow and takes ink text on top (welcome panel, urgent row, agenda arrow chip, .h1 .pop highlight).
Contrast
ink on paper 16.7:1 · paper on ultra 10.6:1 · ink on pop 16.9:1 · paper on ultra-2 7.4:1 · ultra on paper (text) 10.6:1 · all AAA. ink-soft (rgba .72) 7.4:1 AAA · ink-faint (rgba .60) 4.9:1 AA.
Signatures
Tight grid + hairline rules on bios. Mono numerals as structural device throughout. Bright neon-yellow welcome panel and deep-navy pocket page give the brochure two saturated "feature" moments — yellow warmth on one side, navy gravity on the other. Headline accent words sit inside ultramarine swatches with cream text. A second .pop highlight class is available for neon-yellow chips with dark ink.
Boxes
Centella panel pattern: 8px radius, hard edges, no inner frame. Urgent items use neon-yellow fill with ink text. Practical info uses a 2-column grid of boxes; full-width boxes flag the things that should not be missed.
Quotes
Three placements — thought piece (right, deep-ink box with neon-yellow edge), bios (right, ultramarine fill with cream text), conduct (left, deep-ink box). Quotes are an active design element here, not decoration.
What participants will feel
Now, not nostalgia — and not solemn. The 20-year mark as momentum and warmth, not monument. Foundation rigor in the navy and the warm paper; high-voltage moments from the neon-yellow.
