1
Confidentiality
What is shared in the room stays in the room. Names, attributions, and specifics do not leave with you.
2
Generous attention
Listen before responding. Assume the speaker has spent more time on the question than you have.
3
Disagreement, not disrespect
Push hard on the idea, never on the person. Your strongest critique should land as a gift.
4
One mic at a time
No side conversations during plenaries. The room only works if everyone is in it.
5
Care for the convening
Phones away, devices down, presence on. Twenty years has bought us the right to be undistracted.

Option D — the eleven-color compass

Direction
Hunt-branded · the “Compass.” Logo-led, drawing from an eleven-color palette. The cover dots, the welcome band, the bio-photo wheel and the pocket band carry all eleven; sections cycle through a curated subset (teal welcome, coral Giovanna, purple thought piece, green & mint bios, teal map, red practical, orange conduct).
Type
Open Sans only — light (300) for editorial display (cover, h1, signatures, marker, conduct numerals, quote glyphs), regular (400) for body and italics, semibold (600/700) for eyebrows, names, urgent labels. Negative tracking on display sets gives the lightweight cuts editorial weight.
Palette
Ten compass colors. White paper (#FFFFFF) and Hunt brown ink (#3E1913, body at 15.5:1 AAA). Compass: #0581A9 teal, #8ED1FC sky, #7BDCB5 mint, #00D084 green, #FCB900 yellow, #FF6900 orange, #CF2E2E red, #EB5D85 coral, #F78DA7 pink, #9B51E0 purple. Brights are used as fills and decorative glyphs only; small text uses AA-clearing deep variants (e.g. #035C7A teal, #0E5A8C sky, #0E7C4F mint, #007A4D green, #6F5000 yellow, #A24300 orange, #931F1F red, #B33D5F coral, #A8466B pink, #6F2BAA purple).
Compass system
Eleven dots on the cover. Eleven stripes on the welcome panel (vertical) and the pocket page (horizontal). Eleven segments in the bio-photo wheel. Section markers carry one color each.
Signatures
Swanee Hunt logo as cover device and 16px inner-page mark. The 11-stripe compass band on the welcome panel and the pocket page. Open Sans Light Italic carries pull-quotes and signatures; Open Sans Semibold uppercase carries eyebrows, labels, folios.
Boxes
Single-rule frames in Hunt brown at 16% opacity, with a 4px left rule in the section’s compass color. Quote boxes default to coral; .alt quotes use purple, with .orange and .blue variants available.
Practical (urgent)
White text on red (#CF2E2E) — passes AA without losing the alarm-color read.
What participants will feel
Continuity with Hunt’s digital identity. Reads as a contemporary foundation report — institutional weight from the logo and ink, expressive variety from the compass.
Discipline
Eleven colors is a wide palette; the discipline that holds it together is: brown ink everywhere, white paper everywhere, brights as fills only, deep variants for any text smaller than display.
