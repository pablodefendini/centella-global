Conduct18
1
Confidentiality
What is shared in the room stays in the room. Names, attributions, and specifics do not leave with you.
2
Generous attention
Listen before responding. Assume the speaker has spent more time on the question than you have.
3
Disagreement, not disrespect
Push hard on the idea, never on the person. Your strongest critique should land as a gift.
4
One mic at a time
No side conversations during plenaries. The room only works if everyone is in it.
5
Care for the convening
Phones away, devices down, presence on. We've earned the right to be undistracted.

Option A — Revised · at a glance

What changed
(1) New program title — "Prime Movers: Meeting the Moment". (2) Cover rebuilt as a static atmospheric graphic — two layered radial blooms create a diagonal energy axis (warm orange in the lower-left, amber in the upper-right), with a single subtle SVG arc tracing an implied trajectory. Solid “MOVERS” in navy, italic Cormorant theme line below. No echo trails, no speed lines, no arrows — every motion cue is a static gradient or path, print-faithful. (3) Color palette borrowed from Option C — sunny cream paper, WWP navy ink (no heavy black anywhere), punchy orange as the primary accent fill, amber as the secondary pop. (4) The dark-ink quote box was replaced with Option C's pull-quote treatment — paper ground, double rule top + single rule below, oversized italic open-quote glyph in deepened orange. (5) Typography: Fira Sans throughout (a single family, full weight range) with Cormorant Garamond italic as the second voice for accents and the open-quote glyph. (6) All eyebrow-style mini-labels (small uppercase letterspaced category tags) stripped — section labels appear as italic Cormorant in deepened orange, or are dropped entirely so headings lead directly.
What didn't change
Option A's facing-spread layout, modular bio grid, hairline-rule bio rows, numeral structural device, the urgent practical row, the agenda-pocket spread, and the Fira Sans type stack — all preserved.
Direction
Contemporary / progressive, warmed and brightened. The modern look that won approval, now in orange-and-blue instead of ultramarine-and-yellow.
Type pairing
Fira Sans (one family, full weight range — 400 for body, 600–800 for display). Cormorant Garamond used in one place only — the oversized italic open-quote glyph that opens every pull-quote, a single C-derived ornament inside an otherwise Fira Sans system.
Palette
R-* tokens. Sunny cream paper (#FFFAEC) and warm peach (#FFE9C8) for box fills. WWP navy (#1B3A52) for ink — body text passes WCAG AAA on cream. Punchy orange (#FF7A1F) carries accent labels, cover band, pocket page, em-phrase chips; deepened orange (#A33D0E) is the accent-text variant for cream grounds. Amber-gold (#FFC130) is the secondary fill — welcome panel, urgent row, agenda arrow chip; deepened amber (#7A5000) is its on-cream text variant.
Fill patterns
Both bright fills now take navy text on top — orange on cover band, accent module, panel--accent, pocket page; amber on welcome panel, urgent row, agenda arrow chip. No more cream-on-deep-fill; every "feature" moment is bright + navy.
Contrast
Navy on cream 11.4:1 AAA · navy on orange 4.7:1 AA (large display) · navy on amber 7.3:1 AAA · orange-deep on cream 6.2:1 AA · amber-deep on cream 6.8:1 AA · cream on orange 5.9:1 AA. ink-soft (rgba .78) on cream ~9:1 AAA · ink-faint (rgba .60) on cream ~5:1 AA.
Signatures
Tight grid + hairline rules on bios. Numerals in Fira Sans throughout. The cover device is the word "MOVERS" in solid Fira Sans 800 floating on a layered radial gradient that implies a diagonal trajectory. Two saturated feature moments — orange (gravity) and amber (warmth) — bracket the book.
Quotes
Single quote system, borrowed from Option C: top double-rule + bottom single-rule, centered italic text, oversized italic open-quote glyph in deepened orange. The bios-spread quote inverts the same shape onto an orange ground with navy text. Replaces A's original dark-ink quote box entirely.
What participants will feel
The same modern movement that won approval — but warmer. Orange + navy reads as celebration with weight; cream paper reads as event, not corporate. Heavy black is gone everywhere.
